Places I Love

Traveling has shaped who I am — here are some of my favorite spots

Why I Love Traveling

Growing up bilingual in a Latino household, travel always felt natural to me. Getting to know new places, trying different foods, and meeting people from all walks of life is something I genuinely treasure. Working with travel companies like Wander Camp and Acanela Expeditions only deepened that love.

Top Destinations on My List

Some places I've been lucky to visit or dream of visiting one day:

  • Peru – Machu Picchu has been a dream since I was a kid
  • Spain – I want to put my Spanish to use in Madrid and Barcelona
  • Mexico City – The food, the culture, the art scene — amazing
  • Japan – I'm fascinated by the mix of tradition and modern life
  • Portugal – The people there speak a mix of Spanish and something totally unique
